My Experience with Whitehat JR

WhiteHat Jr. is an Online learning platform that give 1:1 classes to kids.
WhiteHat Jr. have 3 different type of courses that you can sign for. One for 1 class ,
other is for 48 classes and the the last one is for 144 classes.
The course fee is based on per class.
WhiteHat Jr. conduct these classes on their own Coding platform
You have to book the class in advance based on your preferred time slot.
If you want to change the time slot you can do so 24 hrs prior.

Parent's Review

My initial impressions are that they are really good. For the initial classes, which my daughter is still in, its completely image based.
This means the child doesn’t have to write the code but drag and drop the code that’s already written. This gives an easy entrance into coding world without having to worry about writing textual bases coding.
The coding classes are more of game based with point system to encourage the kids to attempt.
I haven’t found any fault yet and hope I don’t find any in the near future.

White hat junior is the best platform for the kids to learn coding logic.
The curriculum is designed in such a way that at early age group ranges from 5–9 years kids would develop logical thinking, they will start Brainstorming which is actually important to understand coding concepts.
Curriculum is totally based on Pragmatic approach, which makes it unique , so kids will learn by doing.
As per the curriculum, for the kids of age 9 years onwards, Students will lead towards the text based coding.
Fast pace of changing Technology is the reality, this course helps to prepare them for future,
Ultimately the course will develop the following traits in kids, which are actually in demand for IT Industries.
